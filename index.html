Opening Hours

Delivery

Take a journey through our website and discover an array of tempting treats crafted with love and care. From exquisitely decorated cakes to mouthwatering pastries, artisan bread, irresistible cookies, and more, there's something for every palate and every event.

We are open Sunday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 8:30 PM. Whether you're an early bird or a night owl, you'll find us ready to serve you with a smile. Come visit us and experience our warm hospitality and delicious treats.

Our delivery process is crafted to ensure your orders arrive promptly and in pristine condition. Once your order is received through our online platform or via phone call, our dedicated team swings into action. For deliveries within the valley, we offer a flat rate of Rs 100.
